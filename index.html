Frequently asked questions

Is QuickPNG free to use?
Yes, QuickPNG is completely free with no sign-up required. There are no watermarks, no limits, and no hidden fees.
Are my images uploaded to a server?
No. QuickPNG runs entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device — all processing is done locally using AI.
How does the background removal work?
QuickPNG uses a pre-trained AI segmentation model (ONNX Runtime) that runs locally in your browser via WebAssembly. It identifies the foreground subject and removes the background automatically.
What image formats are supported?
QuickPNG accepts JPG, PNG, and WebP images. The result is always exported as a transparent PNG file.
Why does the first image take longer?
On first use, QuickPNG downloads the AI model (about 5–10MB). This is cached by your browser, so subsequent images process much faster.
